[Kotlin](https://kotlinlang.org/) is the new JVM language sensation that's sweeping nations. It's elegant, concise, easy-to-learn and - most importantly: very, very, very pragmatic. This pragmatism was born of deep collaborations with various communities (like server-side JVM-based workloads and mobile Android-based workloads) and their use cases. Join me, a Kotlin Google Developer Expert and the first Spring Developer Advocate, and we'll look at why Spring Boot and Kotlin were made for each other!
